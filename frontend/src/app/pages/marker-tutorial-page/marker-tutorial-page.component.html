Choose dataset
Choose dataset Step 1

First, choose dataset of the data you want to label. After that you will be taken to marker page.
Find anomaly
Find anomaly Step 2

Use the slider on your right to search for anomalies on the picture. Remember that by default you are given only a part of the scan. To download rest of the images, use the slider to go to the end of available view.

If you haven't found any anomalies on the scan, click 'Skip scan' button to get a new scan for labelling`.
Label it
Label it Step 3

Draw rectangle on the image that you find interesting. Using the slider on the right, move to another slices and draw additional rectangle. Collection of such selections will create a single 3D annotation. MedTagger will be able to interpolate such rectangles to prepare a complex 3D structure.

Remember: In case you find multiple anomalies, annotate them separately to create multiple labels.

In this example we are showing how to label fragments of patient ribs.

Send label
Send label Step 4

After creating label, click 'Send label' button. You'll still have option of further labeling the scan after clicking this button.

After sending all of the labels, please click 'Next scan' button to draw another scan for labelling.
